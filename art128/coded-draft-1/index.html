KAIMUKI DRY GOODS, LTD.

Serving Hawaii's fashion needs since 1926.

About Us

Originally owned by Mr. and Mrs. Takao Ozawa, Kaimuki Dry Goods first opened their doors in 1926. The store had been passed on from generation to generation. Through the years they have transitioned from a general dry goods store into the fabric store we know and love today.
